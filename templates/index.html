About This Project

This English to Braille Translator is Melinda Yuan's BCA Invention Convention project, created with a mission to help people in need. Braille is a tactile writing system used by people who are blind or visually impaired, allowing them to read and write independently.

By converting digital text to Braille, this tool helps bridge the gap in accessibility and empowers the blind and visually impaired community to access written information more easily.
